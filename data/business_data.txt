A: Haan, hum government approved generic medicines bhi rakhte hain. Generic medicines branded medicines jaisi hi quality ki hoti hain lekin kaafi sasti hoti hain. Pharmacist se poochh sakte hain.

Q: Kya aap expired medicines vapas lete ho?

Q: Insurance se medicines milti hain kya?

Q: WhatsApp pe order kar sakte hain kya?
